  • Solar Wind Speed: The speed at which charged particles from the Sun, primarily electrons and protons, flow through space, influencing space weather and Earth's magnetic field.

  • Solar Flare Intensity: A measure of the brightness and energy release of sudden bursts of radiation from the Sun's surface, which can disrupt satellite communications and pose hazards to astronauts.

  • Geomagnetic Storm (Kp Index): A scale (ranging from 0 to 9) that quantifies the disturbance in Earth's magnetic field caused by solar activity, indicating the potential impact on technology and navigation systems.
